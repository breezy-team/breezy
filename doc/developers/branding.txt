Breezy branding
===============

``Breezy`` (with that capitalisation) is the name of the project and our current implementation.

``breezy`` is the name of the Python package.

``brz`` is the command line tool.

``Bazaar`` is the name of the old project, as well as the name of the formats.

``bzr`` is the name of the old binary.

Slogan: "Like bzr, but with more b-s in"
